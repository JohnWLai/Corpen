About Us

Corpen Real Estate Group is an independent real estate brokerage navigating the California market. We take a personalized approach to buying, selling, and leasing any type of residential, commercial, or investment property throughout the state. We build our reputation on what matters: honesty, integrity, and results.

Our real estate professionals have more than 20 years of experience in the industry. Our team reflects what makes our communities so unique. With diverse backgrounds in law, technology, design, education, and life sciences, our ability to connect with the people that drive every region's success is unparalleled. We understand what it truly means to live in our communities.

Our Residential Practice

Corpen Real Estate Group is proud to take a personal, results-driven approach to buying and selling residential real estate. As an independent brokerage, our clients get the most out of our extensive network and resources to leverage traditional and innovative strategies to meet the needs of our buyers and sellers.

Buyer Representation

Our active buyer representation services are specifically designed to help you understand the home buying process, analyze key market insights, and identify properties that will ultimately become a home you’ll love.

  • Information: We’ll use our multiple listings systems and networks to provide you wtih access to every property in any regional market.
  • Education: We’ll be there with you through every step of the process, from defining the search to deciding on the perfect home.
  • Negotiation: We’ll make every effort to negotiate the best value for you from offer to final closing.

Seller Representation

When you’re ready to sell, our seller representation services optimize pricing, marketing, and related professional services to drive a personalized go-to-market strategy that ensures the process is successful.

  • Evaluation: We’ll survey the entire market and analyze all available information to price the property accurately and intelligently.
  • Coordination: We’ll handle the marketing campaigns to achieve the highest price possible in the shortest amount of time.
  • Champion: We’ll do everything we can to advocate for you and your best interests throughout the process.

Our Commercial Practice

Corpen Real Estate Group's commitment to the well-being of the region includes our commercial real estate services to a variety of businesses. Our clients are investors, entrepreneurs, restauranteurs, small business owners, and well-established companies that want to contribute to California's vibrant economy. With our team's business experience and instincts in mind, our commercial practice is focused on thoroughly understanding a client's needs to make every transaction a success.

Targeted Search

We listen to our clients and understand their goals and objectives to exhaustively search for the right properties to further their interests. ​

Business Strategy

We help evaluate the potential and fit of various commercial properties as personal and business investments for the short and long term.

Active Negotiation

​We look to secure the best contractual terms for our clients from the real estate perspective, given their business and other financial considerations.
